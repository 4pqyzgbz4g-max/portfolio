Portfolio Website Proposal

Purpose of the Website

The purpose of this portfolio website is to present my professional profile as a casino dealer. The site will help potential employers evaluate my skills, experience, and qualifications for positions in casinos and gaming establishments.

Target Audience

The target audience includes casino managers, recruiters, gaming supervisors, and hiring personnel in the casino industry.

Required Portfolio Components

Based on industry research, an effective employment portfolio should include:

  • A home page with a professional introduction.
  • An experience page showcasing relevant training or simulations.
  • A skills page listing technical and customer service abilities.
  • A resume page with downloadable PDF.
  • A contact page with clear communication options.

Research Sources

According to Indeed Career Guide, job portfolios should highlight skills, experience, and a clear professional summary. Source: Indeed Career Guide
